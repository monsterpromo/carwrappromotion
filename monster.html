ABOUT US


Monster Energy is an energy drink introduced by Hansen Natural Company in April 2002. There are 34 different drinks under the Monster brand in North America, including its core Monster Energy line, Java Monster, Extra Strength, Import, Rehab and Muscle Monster and each one is flavoured uniquely to suit your taste. Monster Energy is advertised mainly through sponsorship of sporting events, including motocross, BMX, mountain biking, snowboarding, skateboarding, car racing, speedway, and also through sponsorship of esports events and now we are bringing it to colleges across the country.

COLLEGE JOBS


As of September 2021, Monster will be visiting 80% of all colleges across the states and we need students and staff for marketing purpose. The marketing strategy is to cover your vehicles: cars, trucks, bikes or bicycles with our printed decals and you can drive around carrying out your usual activity every day hereby making more people aware to our brand and products.

We are offering


  • $700 / Month for car and truck wraps
  • $500 for bikes wraps
  • $300 for bicycles

This marketing program is for a period of 3 months and we will only be selecting the first 100 people from each college

If you’re interested in the job and working with us. Pls fill out the questionnaire below August 29 2022 to October 15 2022
